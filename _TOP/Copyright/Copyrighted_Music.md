# My dream

My dream back in December 2009 was a URL convention for any time-indexed media asset / frame-indexed media asset. So a comic book frame, book page, film frame, music video frame / lyric entry point, song.

That a unique "recording" of a song, not just the song, but the performance. As there are remixes, there are live performances vs. studio recordings, these multiple choices have been going on for the common person since the 1950's / 1960's. 

So even if you had paid for a library, you had purchased tracks on Apple iTunes store, or you had a YouTube Premium subscription that turned off advertising messages.. you could seamlessly play songs from different artists in a mostly gapless way.

I did a huge amount of experimenting on this. As I wanted to be able to play YouTube songs indexed at certain start points and end points to create the collage art style of War and Peace in the Global Village 1968 book. And the whole multi-media March 1967 The Medium is the Massage project... which was a music album, magazine, book, film, etc. SEE_ALSO https://www.TheMediumIsTheMassAge.com

But I've had so many mental breakdowns I've had to shed things in my mind, often in very unpleasant ways. 

My dream was that I could publish a kind of URL / CSV / playlist that played mostly gapless without advertising being inserted.

start-point, end-point, media identifier, volume normalize, visual placement       

And even parallel for fade-in fade-out and multi-screen

Back in 2007 or so, I did a rather exhaustive search for any existing projects that were attempting to do this, I haven't done one recently as I have so much brain burnout and brain damage.

Put simply.. making a public statement... I'm not TRYING to promote YouTube or get people to purchase YouTube Premium to get an advertising-free experience.  Sharing tracks to Africa with my wife I know first-hand how people don't get the same YouTube premium options even if they have the money, and tracks are blocked in other nations, bitrot issues, etc.

The bitrot is sort of part of the ARG itself. Not in a good way, but it does draw attention to what Nina Paley is saying.

It's so exhausting how many years of my mind have been devoted to experimenting and trying to describe the very brain damage Nina Paley discusses. Tower of Babel ARG I've had going too. Brain damage is a big thing I think the Tower of Babel metaphor is describing.
